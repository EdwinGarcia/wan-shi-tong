# 🦉 Wan Shi Tong

> *"You think you're the first person to believe their war was justified? Countless others have come into my library seeking weapons or weaknesses, only to use that knowledge for destruction and violence."*
>
> — Wan Shi Tong, *Avatar: The Last Airbender*

A personal collection of prompts, agents, skills, and other AI-adjacent
artifacts I've built and want to share. Named after the spirit owl who hoards
all knowledge under the desert, minus the part where he buries the library
when humans misuse it. The irony is not lost on me about this being an AI repo as humans will inevitable misuse it.

Like Wan Shi Tong's library, everything here is offered freely. Unlike his
library, you don't have to bring me a new book to enter (though contributions
are welcome).

---

## What's inside

### Prompts

System prompts and agent instructions designed to be pasted into a frontier
LLM (Claude, GPT, Gemini, etc.).

- **[`prompts/fiduciary/`](prompts/fiduciary/)** — A retirement-planning
  fiduciary. Sit down with an AI, hand it your numbers, and get an
  evidence-grounded Bogleheads-philosophy review of where you stand and what
  to consider next. Not financial advice; a thinking partner.

*(More to come.)*

## Repo conventions

Each artifact lives in its own subfolder under a top-level category
(`prompts/`, `agents/`, `skills/`, etc.) and has its own `README.md`
explaining:

- What it does
- When to use it
- How to use it (which model, what to paste, what to expect)
- Any prerequisites or companion resources
